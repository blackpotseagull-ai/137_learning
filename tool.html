1-3-7 Review Tool

How to Use the 1-3-7 Method

1. First Review (After 1 Day)

Review the material for the first time the day after you learn it. This is the most critical step to strengthen the initial memory trace.

2. Second Review (After 3 Days)

Review the material again three days after the *initial* learning session. This helps transition the memory from short-term to long-term.

3. Third Review (After 7 Days)

The final review happens seven days after the *initial* session. This solidifies the knowledge for long-term retention.
